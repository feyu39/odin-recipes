Hong Shao Rou

Ingredients

  • Pork, Ginger, Soy Sauce, Dark Soy Sauce, Scallions
  • Garlic, Rock Candy, Staranese, Cinnamon, Chile

Instructions:

  1. Cut pork belly into bite sized pieces
  2. Place into pan with oil and saute
  3. Put one staranese, 2 small red hot chile and saute till white, then place in a seperate dish
  4. Add cinnamon stick, and rock candy into pan, wait till it melts on low heat
  5. Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, cooking wine, meet, ginger, garlic, and saute
  6. Turn to the lowest heat, add water, cover for 50 mins
  7. Enjoy!
